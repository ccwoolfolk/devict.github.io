Support

Membership in our organization is free as are most of our events. To keep our services free we depend on donations from the community. To that end there are multiple ways to support us.

Donate Via PayPal

If you have a PayPal account and wish to make either a one-time or monthly donation you can do that here: PayPal will collect 2.9% plus $0.30 of your donation.

Donate Via Patreon

If you do not have a PayPal account you can create a recurring donation via Patreon. The fees they take are higher than PayPal but you don’t have to have a PayPal account.

To donate visit devICT on Patreon.

Referral Codes

If you were planning to use Digital Ocean you can try to sign up using our referral code.
